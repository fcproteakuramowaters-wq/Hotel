Ikad Hotel Yaba

Welcome to IKAD Hotel Yaba — formerly known as Coolio Hotel & Suites — where modern comfort meets refined hospitality in the heart of Lagos. Conveniently located along Borno Way in Yaba, our hotel offers a peaceful escape amid one of Lagos's most vibrant and connected neighborhoods.

Each of our well-appointed rooms is designed with your comfort in mind, featuring elegant furnishings, cozy bedding, and modern amenities to make your stay both restful and memorable. Whether you are visiting for business, academics, or leisure, IKAD Hotel provides the perfect blend of convenience, quality, and warmth.

Enjoy seamless access to nearby landmarks such as the University of Lagos, Yaba College of Technology, and Tejuosho Market — all just minutes away. At IKAD Hotel Yaba, we carry forward the legacy of Coolio Hotel with a renewed commitment to excellence, style, and guest satisfaction.
